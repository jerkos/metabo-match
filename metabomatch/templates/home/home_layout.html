Many softwares...

More and more softwares are released everyday to tackle biological data processing (Big Data problem). Especially metabolomics data coming from mass spectrometry. Machines are constantly evolving and community adapt their workflow to get more insight in the biological problem they study.

Which one may fit best your needs in terms of quality and performance ?

Many softwares have common functionalities, they can do it well or not, they can do it fast or not. We aim to guide bioinformaticians, researchers, clinicians to find the best tool to fit their needs quickly with confidence. We tried to class softwares in different part of the cassical metabolomics analysis pipeline and attribute mark based on rigourous criteria so that user can identify the best tools established by the community.

Helping you in that task !

On this website, you will be able to:

  • find free metabolomics softwares for each step of the standard metabolomics pipeline analysis.
  • get some informations on it
  • find the best softwares for your needs thanks to our notation system based on the community feedback.
  • be aware of the feedback on each tool of the community
  • Share your favorites scripts
  • Discuss about softwares algorithm, installtion problem... Why not new idea and crowdsourcing ?
